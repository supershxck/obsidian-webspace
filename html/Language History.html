  • Development: One of the earliest known written languages, used in Mesopotamia.

  • Development: Hieroglyphic writing system used in Ancient Egypt.

  • Link: [[Akkadian]]

  • Development: Ancestral language of Sino-Tibetan languages, including Chinese and Tibetan.

  • Hittite (~1600 BCE)

  • Development: Indo-European language used in Anatolia, one of the earliest attested Indo-European languages.

Influences and Interactions

  • Greek Influences: Greek influenced Latin, which in turn influenced many European languages.
  • Arabic Influences: Arabic influenced many languages in the Islamic world, including Persian, Turkish, and Urdu.
  • Latin Influences: Latin was the foundation for Romance languages like Spanish, French, Italian, Portuguese, and Romanian.
  • Germanic Influences: Old Norse and Old High German influenced English and other Germanic languages.
