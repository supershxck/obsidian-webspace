Personality Psychology

What is there to know about personality? The more you understand yourself and others the better your plans and success and the less frustration and disappointment. Differences help us solve different problems. All benefit from diversity. 

How do I survive with partial knowledge? Start with complete resolution of the world. A map with not a lot of detail. Low resolution.  When things fail, the low res might not have enough info to get you where you need to be.  This can produce anxiety but also show possibilities.  Crisis can be an opportunity.  Usually negative emotion is experienced before possibilities unfold.  Multiple framing systems with scaffolding from other people as well as games that allow social interaction psychologically.  Personality provides the framework within which you perceive relevant facts.  The nature of perception, facts, and values.  Attentional focus is limited by your goals.  You pick your targets and only give energy to what is part of your determined goal. 

What is it determining what you’re aiming at? Aiming is necessary to see To aim is to focus  And to focus is to focus intently  What you see is a reflection of your value structure. You aim at what you need or want and that determines what you see, hear, sense..

How do you orient yourself in society?  Constraints on how to process the world without it being overwhelming Motivations set goals and emotions regulate the drive towards that goal 

Key Takeaways Personality traits are low resolution solutions to complex problems.  Humans use structures to process the world and not be overwhelmed These structures can be divided into emotional and motivational systems
