Conscientiousness

Industriousness & Orderliness

Industrious

●  Carry out my plans
●  Don’t waste time
●  Don’t find it difficult to get down to work
●  Finish what I start
●  Get things done quickly (don’t postpone)
●  Always know what I’m doing
●  Not easily distracted  ●  Don’t mess things up ●  Put my mind on the task at hand ●  Don’t postpone decisions

Orderly

●  Tidy
●  Like order
●  Bothered by messy people
●  Want everything to be just right
●  Bothered by disorder
●  Like routine
●  See that rules are observed
●  Want every detail taken care of
●  Follow a schedule

Conscientious

●  Conscientious vs Negligent  ●  careful vs careless ●  reliable vs undependable ●  well-organized vs disorganized  ●  well-disciplined vs weak willed ●  persevering vs quitting

Conscientious Industrious ppl value duty, diligence, and focus. 

Conscientious orderly ppl value organizational ability, concentration on detail, insistence on borders and boundaries. 

Conscientiousness, particularly orderliness, is associated with a higher disgust sensitivity. Disgust is a kind of negative emotion but is not related to neuroticism.
