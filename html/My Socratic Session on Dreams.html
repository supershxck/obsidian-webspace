My opening question that I’m leaning towards currently is: What are dreams?  What is intuition?  What is imagination? What is consciousness? What is subconsciousness? Is there a collective unconscious?

After attempted definition and shared experiences Ask, "What is consciousness?" and "How does our understanding of being awake relate to what happens when we dream?" "How does imagination influence our dreams?" and "In what ways are dreams a form of imagination?" Ask participants to reflect on their own dreams or imaginative experiences and how they might relate to their subconscious thoughts or desires. Consider the implications of understanding dreams, consciousness, and imagination better. How can this understanding affect creativity, problem-solving, or personal growth?
