Summary Insight

From Sir Robert Walpole’s establishment of prime ministerial precedent to Rishi Sunak’s contemporary leadership, the UK premiership reflects evolving balances between monarchy, Parliament, and public expectations — tracing a lineage of ideological shifts from Whig constitutionalism through Conservative, Liberal, Labour, and neoliberal eras.
