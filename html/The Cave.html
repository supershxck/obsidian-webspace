The cave  Has a fire at the entrance  It dances To protect the cave from those who dare to enter But those who are fireproof  Learn to dance w the flame To enter past the flame  The darkness grows Until the walls begin to shine light  W green crystals  And a dark brown/black bear prowls around The claws are sharp And blood drips from its mouth  It’s eyes glow like lava And within the bear is the spirit that breaks free to shatter the cave and become something never seen before

The bear enjoys its cage
