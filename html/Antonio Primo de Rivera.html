  • Birth and Early Life: Antonio Primo de Rivera, born on April 24, 1903, in Madrid, [[Spain]], was the eldest son of Miguel Primo de Rivera, a Spanish military dictator.
  • Political Career: Antonio founded the Spanish Falange, a fascist political organization, in 1933. The Falange advocated for a totalitarian state, nationalism, and anti-communism.
  • Spanish Civil War: He was arrested in 1936 and executed by the Republican government during the Spanish Civil War. His death made him a martyr for the Nationalist cause, and his ideology influenced Francoist Spain.
