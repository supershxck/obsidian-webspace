Below is a structured reference for all 78 Tarot cards. Each entry offers:

  1. Affirmations (1–3 concise affirmations)

  2. Symbolic Words (4–6 keywords that capture the card’s essence)

  3. Deity Associations (1–3 deities from various mythologies who embody the card’s energy)

Major Arcana

0. The Fool

• Affirmations:

  1. “I trust life’s journey.”

  2. “I move forward with courage and wonder.”

• Symbolic Words: innocence, spontaneity, adventure, optimism, potential

• Deity Associations: Pan (Greek), Hermes (Greek)

• Affirmations:

  1. “I manifest my desires through focused will.”

  2. “I am the channel of divine creativity.”

• Symbolic Words: power, skill, manifestation, concentration, action

• Deity Associations: [[Hermes]] (Greek), [[Thoth]] (Egyptian)

• Affirmations:

• Symbolic Words: mystery, intuition, subconscious, inner-knowledge

• Deity Associations: [[Isis]] (Egyptian), [[Persephone]] (Greek), [[Hecate]] (Greek)

  2. “I open myself to receive and to give.”

• Deity Associations: [[Demeter]] (Greek), [[Hathor]] (Egyptian)
