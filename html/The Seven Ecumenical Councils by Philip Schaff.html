This volume contains the writings of the Seven Ecumenical Councils. Spanning four centuries, they are:

[[the First Council of Nicaea]] [[the First Council of Constantinople]] [[the Council of Ephesus]] [[Council of Chalcedon]] [[the Second Council of Constantinople ]] [[the Third Council of Constantinople ]] [[the Second Council of Nicaea]]

The Nicene and Post-Nicene Fathers are comprehensive in scope, and provide keen translations of instructive and illuminating texts from some of the great theologians of the Christian church. These spiritually enlightening texts have aided Christians for over a thousand years, and remain instructive and fruitful even today!
