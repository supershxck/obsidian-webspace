Actionable Frameworks

1️⃣ Diversification Matrix

Map your resources across asset classes (public equities, private equity, real estate, alternatives) and geographies to mitigate concentration risk. Use a 5×5 grid to score exposure vs. strategic importance.

2️⃣ Governance Scorecard

Create a three‑tier oversight model:

• Tier 1: Board/Steering Committee (strategic direction)

• Tier 2: Executive Management (execution & compliance)

• Tier 3: Audit & Ethics (independent review)

3️⃣ Long‑Horizon Roadmap

Draft a 10‑year strategic plan segmented into 3 phases (0–3 years: foundation; 3–7 years: scale; 7–10 years: sustainability). Assign quarterly review checkpoints (akin to “ephemeris updates”).
