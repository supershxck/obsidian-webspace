**Who Is Guy Ballard?**

  

**Introduction**

  

Guy Ballard (1878–1939) was an American businessman, engineer, and the founder of the _I AM_ Activity, a spiritual movement centered around the concept of personal empowerment, spiritual transformation, and the teachings of ascended masters, particularly Saint Germain. Ballard’s life and teachings have had a significant impact on New Age spirituality, and he is best known for his claimed encounters with the ascended masters and for popularizing their messages of self-realization and the power of the “I AM Presence.”

**1. Early Life and Background**

  

**1.1 Birth and Early Education**

  

Guy Ballard was born on February 28, 1878, in the United States. He was raised in a fairly conventional American family, and his early education reflected the values of the time, with a focus on practical skills and an interest in science and engineering. Ballard was described as an intelligent and ambitious individual, with a strong drive for success.

• **Career Before Spiritual Revelation**

Prior to his spiritual work, Ballard worked as a mining engineer and businessman. He spent time in various professional fields, building a reputation as an intelligent and capable individual. However, despite his material success, Ballard felt a deep inner yearning for spiritual fulfillment and answers to life’s mysteries.

**2. Spiritual Awakening and the Founding of the “I AM” Activity**

  

**2.1 Encounters with Ascended Masters**

  

In the early 1930s, while on a trip to Mount Shasta, California, Ballard claimed to have had a series of spiritual encounters with **ascended masters**, most notably Saint Germain. During these encounters, Saint Germain allegedly imparted to him teachings on divine self-realization, the “I AM Presence,” and the transformative power of the violet flame. These teachings would become the core of what would later be known as the _I AM_ Activity.

  

**Key Claims:**

• **Saint Germain’s Guidance**

According to Ballard, Saint Germain revealed to him profound spiritual truths about humanity’s divine origin and purpose. This guidance included the power of affirmations, the importance of invoking the “I AM Presence,” and the concept of spiritual ascension, where individuals could overcome limitations and attain a higher state of consciousness.

• **The Violet Flame**

One of the central teachings received from Saint Germain was the concept of the **violet flame**, a transformative spiritual energy that purifies negativity, dissolves karma, and accelerates spiritual evolution. Ballard was instructed to share these teachings with the world in order to help others achieve personal transformation.

  

**2.2 Establishing the “I AM” Activity**

  

In 1930, Ballard began to formalize his spiritual experiences by teaching others the principles he had learned from Saint Germain. This eventually led to the founding of the _I AM_ Activity, which gained a following due to its emphasis on self-empowerment, the power of prayer and affirmation, and the connection with ascended masters.

• **Spiritual Practices**

The core of the _I AM_ Activity involved the use of affirmations (such as “I AM divine light”) and decrees (short, powerful statements of spiritual intent) to invoke divine power and accelerate spiritual awakening. The “I AM Presence” was taught as a direct link to the divine essence within each individual.

• **Books and Publications**

Ballard authored several books and writings that outlined the principles of the _I AM_ Activity. These books, including titles like _Unveiled Mysteries_ and _The Magic Presence_, became foundational texts for followers of the movement. These works described his personal encounters with Saint Germain and offered practical spiritual teachings.

**3. Teachings and Philosophical Influence**

  

**3.1 The “I AM Presence” and Divine Self-Realization**

  

One of the central teachings of Ballard’s _I AM_ Activity is the concept of the **“I AM Presence”**, a divine essence believed to reside within every individual. This presence is seen as the direct link to the higher self and the source of all creation. By affirming the “I AM” presence, individuals can align themselves with divine truth, overcome negative thought patterns, and manifest their highest potential.

• **Empowerment Through Affirmation**

The “I AM” affirmation is considered one of the most powerful spiritual tools in the _I AM_ Activity. By repeating positive affirmations such as “I AM abundant,” “I AM healthy,” or “I AM divine wisdom,” followers are encouraged to raise their vibration and attract positive changes into their lives.

  

**3.2 Ascended Masters as Guides**

  

Ballard’s teachings were also heavily influenced by the concept of **ascended masters**—spiritually enlightened beings who have transcended the physical realm and now work from higher planes to guide humanity. Among the most important of these ascended masters was **Saint Germain**, who is said to be the master of the Seventh Ray and associated with transformation, healing, and personal freedom.

• **The Role of Ascended Masters**

Through the use of decrees and prayer, practitioners of the _I AM_ Activity could connect with these masters, receiving guidance and divine assistance in their spiritual journey. In particular, Saint Germain was revered for his teachings on personal alchemy and the transformation of the soul.

**4. The Legacy of Guy Ballard and the “I AM” Activity**

  

**4.1 The Growth of the Movement**

  

The _I AM_ Activity gained momentum during the 1930s and 1940s, attracting followers who were drawn to its promise of spiritual empowerment and transformation. Ballard’s teachings provided a clear, structured approach to spiritual growth, emphasizing both individual responsibility and the importance of divine connection.

• **The Saint Germain Foundation**

After Guy Ballard’s death in 1939, his wife, **Edna Ballard**, continued to promote the _I AM_ Activity through the establishment of the **Saint Germain Foundation**. This organization has worked to preserve and disseminate the teachings of the _I AM_ Activity, publishing books, holding classes, and offering guidance to spiritual seekers.

  

**4.2 Influence on New Age Spirituality**

  

The _I AM_ Activity played a significant role in the development of **New Age spirituality**, particularly in the areas of **self-empowerment**, **healing**, and **manifestation**. The movement’s emphasis on affirmations, the violet flame, and personal transformation has influenced a wide range of spiritual practices, including those in modern metaphysical and esoteric traditions.

• **Connection to Other Esoteric Teachings**

The _I AM_ Activity shares many similarities with other esoteric teachings, such as those found in **Theosophy** and **Ascended Master Teachings**, and has contributed to the popularization of the idea that individuals can access higher spiritual wisdom and guidance through focused intention and personal effort.

**5. Criticism and Controversy**

  

**5.1 Skepticism and Lack of Evidence**

  

Like many spiritual movements, the _I AM_ Activity has been met with skepticism, especially regarding the authenticity of Guy Ballard’s encounters with ascended masters. Critics have questioned the veracity of his claims, particularly since there is no external verification of his mystical experiences.

  

**5.2 Commercialization of the Teachings**

  

There has also been criticism of the commercialization of the _I AM_ Activity, with some accusing the movement of profiting from spiritual teachings through the sale of books, courses, and materials. Critics argue that this commercialization detracts from the movement’s spiritual integrity.

**6. Conclusion**

  

Guy Ballard’s contributions to spiritual thought through the _I AM_ Activity continue to resonate with those seeking empowerment and divine guidance. His teachings on the “I AM Presence,” the violet flame, and the role of ascended masters have had a lasting impact on modern spirituality, especially within the New Age movement. Despite the controversies surrounding the movement, Ballard’s vision of a path to personal transformation and spiritual ascension remains influential in the lives of many who seek a deeper connection with their divine essence.
