**I. Western Europe (40 Figures)**

1. **[[Leonardo da Vinci]]**

• Renaissance genius: painter, inventor, and scientist whose innovations reshaped art and engineering.

**Concluding Reflections**

  

The 15th century was a pivotal era of cultural rebirth, exploration, and political transformation. From the luminaries of Western Europe who defined the Renaissance to the dynamic rulers and scholars of the Islamic world, from the resilient last days of Byzantium to the flourishing courts of East Asia, and from the transformative states of South Asia and Africa to the emerging civilizations in the Americas and Central Asia—the 100 figures outlined above, each encapsulated in double brackets, played vital roles in shaping our world. Their enduring legacies continue to illuminate the pathways of art, science, governance, and spiritual thought, offering rich insights into a century of profound change and interconnected global evolution.
