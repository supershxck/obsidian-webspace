> Do you wish to commune with God in your mind by receiving a perception of that delight which is not enslaved to the senses? Pursue mercy; for when something that is like unto God is found in you, then that holy beauty is depicted by Him. For the whole sum of the deeds of mercy immediately brings the soul into communion with the unity the glory of the Godhead’s splendor.
