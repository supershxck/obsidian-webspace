## Extraversion
### Enthusiasm and Assertive Traits
##### Assertive trait descriptors
- Take charge
- Strong personality 
- Talent for influencing people
- Know how to captivate people
- Lead the way
- See myself as a good leader
- Can talk others into doing things
- Doesn’t hold back opinions
- First to act 
- An assertive personality 
##### Enthusiasm trait descriptors   
- Makes friends easily
- Easy to get to know
- Gets close to others
- Reveals alot about myself
- Warm up quickly to others
- Gets caught up in excitement 
- An enthusiastic person
- Show my feelings when hoppy
- Have alot of fun, laugh alot 

High probability that these are genetically influenced but they can also be influenced by environment 
##### Extraversion trait descriptors
- Sociable vs retiring
- Fun loving vs sober
- Affectionate vs reserved
- Friendly vs aloof
- Spontaneous vs inhibited
- Talkative vs quiet
- Make eye contact
- Drawn to sales and personnel work
- More impulsive 

If your extraverted you tend to discount the future more
Introverts value the future more then the present 
Motivations help establish goals while emotions keep you on track
Positive emotions move you forward toward your goals
Negative emotions can cause you to stop or move backward
Left hemisphere is useful for positive emotion and movement forward
Right hemisphere is useful for negative emotion and uncertainty 
Left brain is also in charge of linguistic usually   
Extraverts generally think that the world’s a pretty good place and that you can get a long way by interacting with other people. They see them as fields of opportunity
