## Five Factor Model
Big 5 was developed in the 1990s and uses a words based approach to determine personality traits 

The Pre-Frontal Cortex is in charge of language capacities and ability to see forward into future, as well as regulate our behavior. 
Dorsolateral helps with abstract problem solving

Big 5 cognitive functions can be assessed reliably and validly, and they did have incremental predictive utility. 

Axiomatic Presuppositions 
1. Human Lexicons, entire vocabulary of language, has captured the essential elements of personality. We basically have all the words needed to describe what we need to about ourselves and others. 
2. There are various ways to get access to that lexical information using questions, phrases, or adjectives. People know enough about their own personality to report on it with accuracy. 

By using questions to probe for the dimensions of personality they have arrived at the conclusion that there are at least 5 dimensions of personality 

1. Extraversion
2. Neuroticism
3. Agreeableness 
4. Conscientiousness
5. Openness to Experience

Each one can be broken into two for higher resolution
- Compassion
- Politeness
- Industriousness
- Orderliness
- Enthusiasm
- Assertiveness
- Withdrawal
- Volatility
- Openness
- Intellect
