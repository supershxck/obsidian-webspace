**Leo Tolstoy (1828–1910): Master of the Epic Novel and Moral Philosopher**

---

**Introduction**

  

Count **Leo Nikolayevich Tolstoy** is hailed as one of literature’s greatest novelists and a profound moral thinker. His sweeping narratives captured the breadth of Russian society while probing existential questions of faith, violence, and the search for meaning.

---

**Early Life & Context**

• **Born:** September 9, 1828, Yasnaya Polyana, Russia

• Raised in aristocratic landowning family; experienced rural peasant life firsthand

• Served in Crimean War (1853–1856), witnessing human suffering that shaped his worldview

---

**Major Works & Themes**

|**Title**|**Year**|**Genre**|**Core Themes**|
|---|---|---|---|
|_War and Peace_|1869|Epic Novel|Historical destiny, free will, collective consciousness|
|_Anna Karenina_|1878|Novel|Love vs social convention, moral consequence|
|_The Death of Ivan Ilyich_|1886|Novella|Existential crisis, authenticity in face of mortality|
|_Resurrection_|1899|Novel|Redemption, social justice, critique of institutional religion|

  

---

**Philosophical & Psychological Dimensions**

|**Dimension**|**Insight**|
|---|---|
|Spiritual Awakening|Turned toward Christian anarchism and pacifism after 1879 crisis|
|Collective Shadow|Exposed societal hypocrisy and violence — advocated nonviolent resistance (Jungian integration)|
|Moral Inquiry|Explored the “meaning of life” through ascetic practice and service|

  

---

**Legacy & Influence**

|**Domain**|**Impact**|
|---|---|
|Literature|Redefined realism and psychological depth in the novel form|
|Nonviolence|Influenced Gandhi, Martin Luther King Jr. on civil disobedience|
|Ethical Thought|Pioneered secular moral philosophy stressing compassion|

**Key Vocabulary**

• **Christian Anarchism:** Rejection of state authority in favor of divine law

• **Asceticism:** Voluntary simplicity to purify intent

• **Moral Realism:** Ethical principles derived from lived experience

• **Collective Consciousness:** Shared social values shaping history
