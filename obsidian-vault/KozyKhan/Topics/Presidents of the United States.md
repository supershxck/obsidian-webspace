**Summary Insights**

• **Two Non‑Consecutive Terms:** [[Grover Cleveland]] uniquely served as both 22nd and 24th President.

• **Longest Tenure:** [[Franklin D. Roosevelt]] (12 years) shaped U.S. response to the [[Great Depression]] and [[World War II]].

• **Historic Firsts:** [[John F. Kennedy]] was the first Catholic president; Barack Obama the first African American president.
